Title
When I led the Google Brain team, we started to build the C++ precursor to TensorFlow called DistBelief. We were excited about the potential of harnessing thou‐ sands of CPUs to train a neural network (for instance, using 16,000 CPUs to train a cat detector on unlabeled YouTube videos). How far deep learning has come since then! What was once cutting-edge can now be done for around $3,000 of cloud com‐ puting credits, and Google routinely trains neural networks using TPUs and GPUs at a scale that was unimaginable just years ago.
